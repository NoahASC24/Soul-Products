It all started with a cube...

Originally, I considered Blender to be a complex software that only professionals used. Eventually, it got to a point where my models were looking terrible without any outside help (using the basic Roblox parts).
My journey to Blender started out ambitious, with the creation of my first person model! Or something...
I got better over time and now use my blender models as assets in making games. To me, it's fun to watch a simple cube transform into something huge.
